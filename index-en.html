About ZEWU

NANJING ZEWU PUBLIC SPACE ART Co., Ltd. is a professional stage art design company and stage engineering company, integrating stage construction, set building, stage art production and stage design-build services. We provide comprehensive stage art solutions and design-build integration, covering stage space design, sculptural arts, and stage machinery installation.

As an experienced live performance stage art company, we have participated in the production of over ten large-scale landscape live performances across China, including "Dongjing Dream", "King's Chariot", "Restart Shanhaijing", "Blooming Jinggang", "China", "Kunlun Appointment", "Dream Homeland", "Chang'E", "Four Seasons Zhouzhuang", and "Baoen Ceremony". We specialize in live performance stage art design, scenic area live performance construction, cultural tourism stage engineering, immersive live performance construction and large-scale live performance production.

We are also a professional film/TV set construction company and film art production company, providing film set construction, TV show set building, variety show stage art design, film location set building, studio stage art design and short drama location construction. In the field of theme park stage engineering, theme park stage art construction and interactive amusement scene production, we have designed and built permanent themed stages, parade floats and various landscape models for multiple Fantawild theme parks across China.
